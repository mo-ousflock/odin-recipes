Description

A grilled cheese sandwich is a simple comfort food made by melting cheese between two slices of buttered bread until golden and crispy.
It is quick to make and has a warm, gooey center with a crunchy exterior.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1-2 slices of cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread.
  2. Place cheese between the unbuttered sides of the bread to form a sandwich.
  3. Heat a pan over medium heat.
  4. Place the sandwich in the pan and cook until the bottom is golden brown.
  5. Flip and cook the other side until golden and the cheese is fully melted.
  6. Remove from the pan, slice, and serve warm.
